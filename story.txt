Story 


Once upon a time, in forest, there lived a monkey on a jamoon tree, which was on the banks of the river. There lived a crocodile and his wife. One day the crocodile came to the banks of the river and rested under tne tree. The kindhearted monkey offered him some fruits. The crocodile came back the next day and asked for some more fruits, as he loved them. As days passed by, the crocodile and monkey became good friends.;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;
